Purpose Of Chamber Of Commerce

The purpose of this chamber of commerce is to advertise business in Petionville town. The overall goal is to promote business interests of the members of the organization and promote the area, attracting additional business and people, give best opporttunity and help visitors and best manners.

The chamber is not a social organization. We are not a branch of the local government. We are here to work for companies and people in our community who want to participate and make a difference in their places of origin.
